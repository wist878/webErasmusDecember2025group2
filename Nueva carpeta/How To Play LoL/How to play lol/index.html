How to Play League of Legends

A beginner-friendly guide to mastering the lanes!

Top Lane

Garen

Darius

Nasus

Malphite

The Top lane is usually home to tanky champions or bruisers who can survive long fights. Your job is to hold the lane, pressure your opponent, and later become a frontline threat or split-pusher. You often play alone, so good wave management and trading are key.

Jungle

Warwick

Amumu

Master Yi

Rammus

The jungler controls objectives like Dragon and Rift Herald, helps lanes early, and determines map tempo. You must farm camps efficiently and look for ganks when enemies are overextended.

Mid Lane

Annie

Lux

Malzahar

Ahri

Mid-lane champions control the center of the map. You usually play mages or assassins. Your job is to push the wave, roam to help other lanes, and provide high damage in fights.

ADC (Bot Lane)

Ashe

Miss Fortune

Caitlyn

Jinx

ADCs deal consistent ranged damage and scale into late game. Your job is to farm safely, position well, and survive team fights. You rely heavily on your Support.

Support

Leona

Yuumi

Soraka

Morgana

Supports protect their ADC, control vision, and set up fights. You don’t need to farm, so your focus is helping your team and using abilities at the right time.

General Tips for All Roles

  • Always ward important areas.
  • Never fight when your abilities are on cooldown.
  • Farm as much as possible — gold wins games.
  • Stick with your team late game.
  • Ping often and communicate.
